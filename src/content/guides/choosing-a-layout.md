ThariKey ships several keyboard layouts so you can type Thaana the way that suits you.

## The layouts

- **Phonetic** — keys map to the Thaana sound they resemble; natural if you think in sounds.
- **Typewriter** — the traditional Thaana typewriter arrangement.
- **Dhives Akuru** — for the historic script.

Each layout is data-driven, and you can remap keys in the app's customizer.

## Switching layouts

Pick your active layout in the ThariKey app. You can switch at any time — the change applies the next
time you type.

Have a layout or tip worth sharing? Guides are open to contributions on
[GitHub](https://github.com/tharikey).

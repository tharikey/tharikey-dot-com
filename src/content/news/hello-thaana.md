---
title: Bringing sanity to Thaana input
date: 2026-06-06
summary: Why ThariKey exists, and what we're building — a Thaana input method, a font library, and the tooling around them.
---

Thaana input on modern systems has been a patchwork for years: scattered layouts, fonts you hunt
down across forums, and no single place that treats Dhivehi typing as a first-class problem.

ThariKey is the attempt to fix that across the board — a fast, data-driven macOS input method
built on a lexicon-free engine, a curated font library you can install in a click, and the
tooling to keep it all honest.

This is the first note. More as the pieces land.
